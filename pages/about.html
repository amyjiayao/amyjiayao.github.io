About Me

Hello, my name is Jiayao Li. I go by Amy. I am a Computer Science and Mechanical Engineering double major senior undergraduate student from Texas A&M University. A little bit about my background, I grew up in Beijing, China. I moved to the U.S. when I was 15 as an exchange student. I lived with a local American host family and attended a year of high school in Corvallis, Oregon. That year of studying abroad introduced me to new perspectives of life and broadened my worldview, which motivated me to come back to the U.S. for college.
Growing up, I have always had a passion for learning. I chose to major in Computer Science and Mechanical Engineering because they are challenging majors and they would open doors for me to solve more problems in real world. I love learning new things and being able to apply my knowledge to solve problems. I aspire to be an independent thinker who would live intentionally and serve selflessly. Regardless of what I do in life, I hope I can always keep my mind sharpened and make positive impacts to the people around me.
